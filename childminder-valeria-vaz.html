Sweet Angels Childcare

Sweet Angels Childcare Service is a home-based childcare with more than 10 years of experience.

We are 2 childminders with NVQ level III in childcare and 4 qualified assistants working in our setting.

Our setting was grade GOOD by OFSTED in our last inspection last November/2016.

We are based at 35, Kingston Avenue, NG317DS.

We work Monday to Friday (except bank holidays) from 6:00am to 8pm.

Please visit our facebook page (@sweetangelschildcare) for more information and feel free to contact us or arrange an informal visit to our setting.

We are able to offer 15/30 hours free childcare for 2/3/4 years old. We accept all childcare vouchers and we are registered for tax free childcare.
