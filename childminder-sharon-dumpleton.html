Sharon Dumpleton’s Childminding service

I aim to provide all my children in my care a safe, caring environment so they can play and grow to be confident independent young people. I provide a wide variety of learning activities e.g. trains, dolls, wooden blocks, puzzles, books, farm animals, dinosaurs, role play food, shopping, post office, puppets, Sewing. I also offer creative play such as painting, Model making, glue, stickers, play dough, clay, baking, Hama beads, Mosaic’s etc. I am very lucky to have a park across the road from my house which we use regularly and we have an enclosed garden covered with Astro turf for the children to play outdoors.
