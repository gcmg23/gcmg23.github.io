My aim is to offer stimulating, happy, flexible childcare in a homely and safe environment where your child will be able to learn through play at their own pace.

I have a wide variety of toys and resources for all ages both inside and outside. We have a large variety of books and games, we have a role play corner, dressing up, construction toys as well as lots of open ended resources.

I also do creative and messy play such as painting, junk modelling, playdough and water and sand play as well as playdough and gloop. Children have free access to drawing materials at all times.

Outside we have ride on toys, blackboard, mud kitchen, guttering for balls and water as well as sand and water play. We also have regular outings to Little Jacks Farm, Diddy Dancers dance class, local parks, Belton house, local cafes etc.

I have been a childminder since 2004 and I enjoy my job very much. I work with an assistant and we both treat the children how we would want out own children to be treated. We love seeing the children grow into confident and independent little people.
