My philosophy

My aim is to provide a warm, welcoming and stimulating environment, build positive relationships with children in my care and make sure their individual needs are met so they can reach their full potential. I hope to offer children a continuity of care as close to their home environment as possible. I work along side with my partner David who is also fully registered with OFSTED as my assistant abd CRB checked to work in this setting.
We like children in our care to feel "part of the family" and thus valued as a family member.
