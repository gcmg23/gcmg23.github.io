Information for childminders

Grantham Childminding Group was set up in the spring of 2004 in order for childminders to have a base of support in their local area. We vary in ages and experience which is what makes our group great, as we all support each other and can share experiences so we are not isolated. Some have been childminding for 30+ years!

We meet up once a week so that we, and the children we care for, can have some social time. During these times we play, chat and do activities with the children.

Join the Childminding group!

If not already a group member, anyone can join. Telephone Elise Webb (07894 631 601) giving her your OFSTED registration number and PACEY membership number if you have one. (By becoming part of a PACEY group, you get a discount on your annual PACEY fees).

Group membership is in two tiers - full or casual.

Full Membership

Cost is £30.00 per year (plus there is a one off fee additional charge of £20 to put all your details for advertising on our website. There after fees for the year are as shown). Fees entitle the member to have an entry on this site plus free entry to the group's daytime drop in session. If you hold a position in the group, Chair person, Treasurer, Secretary, Website co-coordinator or Minder-finder co-coordinator, then your membership will be discount to £20.00 a year.

Casual Membership

Pay £1 per childminder for each visit to our Belton Lane sessions. - please note casual membership does not include entry onto this website.

Marketing ideas

Make sure you keep your details updated on www.lincolnshire.gov.uk/fis (this is where most of the work comes through); you could advertise on your car; put a leaflet up in shops and libraries; or do a local leaflet drop.

Weekly Group Get Togethers

Every Wednesday morning 9am - 11.30am, Belton Lane Children's Centre, Belton Lane School, Grantham. If you are new to childminding and want to make friends and have a support network, come along and join us. We’d love to meet you :) We have activities for the children, snacks, song time and time to chat. For more information on this session, please contact Elise Webb 07894631601 or just turn up.

Days Out & Special Occasions

These are organised by various members of the group over the year. It’s always great when people have ideas to share and step up to organise events for each other, so if you have ideas of something you would like to do please share it with the group. We usually get discount on group outings etc. Events organised are usually confirmed on our closed members only face book page and by email.

Discounts

We all love money off and as childminders you can now get 10% off at Early Learning Centre's either by taking your registration certificate into a store or going to their new site for nurseries etc. and entering your registration number.

Library

As childminders we can have a special library card that allows us to take out more childrens books, and to keep them for longer. Please don't throw away your own card because sometimes they don't let you take out adult books on this card.
